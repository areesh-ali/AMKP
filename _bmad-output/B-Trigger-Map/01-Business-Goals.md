# 01 — Business Goals: AMKP Console

**Created:** 2026-07-16  
**Author:** areesh

## Vision statement

AMKP Console is the Claude-calm human surface for the AMKP knowledge plane: interactive upload and agent work, Evidence-first climax, hard tenancy, DX kit preserved.

## Objectives

| # | Statement | Metric | Target | Tier |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1 | Operators reach first Evidence fast | Time sign-in → Evidence | &lt; 60m | Primary |
| 2 | Isolation is demonstrable | Cross-Tenant leaks | 0 | Primary |
| 3 | Console complements SDK/MCP | DX surfaces retained | Unchanged purpose | Primary |
| 4 | Agentic work is inspectable | Hops visible in UI | 100% agentic Retrieves | Secondary |
| 5 | Eval/policy operable in-product | Eval + policy CAPs | CAP-6/7 met | Secondary |

## CAP mapping

CAP-1 session · CAP-2 admin · CAP-3 documents · CAP-4 retrieve · CAP-5 traces · CAP-6 eval · CAP-7 policy · CAP-8 health · CAP-9 onboarding
